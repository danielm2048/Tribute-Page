Stan Lee

A comic book legend - Excelsior!


Here's a timeline of Stan the Man's life:

1922

Stan Lee is born.

1939

Becomes assistant at Timely Comics.

1941

Stan creates his first superhero The Destroyer.

1942

Enters the US Army.

1945

Returns from military service.

1947

Marries Joan.

1961

Stan creates The Fantastic Four.

1962

Stan creates The Hulk, Spider-Man and Thor.

1963

Stan creates Iron Man and the X-Men.

1966

Stan creates Black Panther.

1974

Gets the Inkport award.

1981

Moves to LA for Marvel's film projects.

1998

Founds Stan Lee Media.

2001

Co-founds POW! Entertainment.

2012

Stan gets the Lifetime Achievement Award.

2015

Stan releases his book Zodiac.

2018

Dies of pneumonia.
